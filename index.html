The Property

Casa Vianey is a beautiful and spacious property nestled in the lush hills of Uvita, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean and the iconic "Whale's Tail" at Marino Ballena National Park.

Our home was designed for families, groups of friends, and nature lovers seeking both adventure and relaxation. Offering 4 bedrooms with 3.5 bathrooms and a private pool. It combines modern comforts with the raw beauty of the Costa Rican jungle.

Whether you're enjoying a morning coffee on the terrace, taking a dip in the pool, or spotting wildlife including sloths, iguanas and macaws, Casa Vianey offers a one-of-a-kind unforgettable Costa Rican experience.

Location & Attractions

Casa Vianey is perfectly situated in Uvita, Costa Rica, allowing you to explore all the wonders of the Southern Pacific Zone.

Nearby Attractions

Marino Ballena National Park

Walk the famous Whale's Tail sandbar and spot humpback whales (seasonal).

Uvita Waterfall & Nauyaca Waterfall

A short hike leads to beautiful, refreshing waterfalls and natural pools. Nauyaca located nearby is also accessible with private transporation available.

Adventure Activities

Ziplining, horseback riding, whale watching, sportfishing and ATV tours are all nearby.

Local Beaches

Playa Uvita, Playa Hermosa, and the surf town of Dominical. Pura Vida!

Gourmet Restaurants

Enjoy the flavors of world famous restaurants such as Seba's & Sibu and other local favorites.

Nightlife

Make new friends and have some late night fun at popular local venues Coco Bongo, La Colonia, and Whale Tail Brewery. A great location for those participating in the Envision festival.
