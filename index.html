The Property

Casa Vianey is a beautiful and spacious property nestled in the lush hills of Uvita, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean and the iconic "Whale's Tail" at Marino Ballena National Park.

Our home is designed for families, groups of friends, and nature lovers seeking both adventure and relaxation. It combines modern comforts with the raw beauty of the Costa Rican jungle.

Whether you're enjoying a morning coffee on the terrace, taking a dip in the pool, or spotting wildlife from the comfort of your living room, Casa Vianey offers an unforgettable Costa Rican experience.

Location & Attractions

Casa Vianey is perfectly situated in Uvita, Costa Rica, allowing you to explore all the wonders of the Southern Pacific Zone.

Nearby Attractions

Marino Ballena National Park

Walk the famous Whale's Tail sandbar and spot humpback whales (seasonal).

Uvita Waterfall

A short hike leads to beautiful, refreshing waterfalls and natural pools.

Adventure Activities

Ziplining, horseback riding, and ATV tours are all nearby.

Local Beaches

Playa Uvita, Playa Hermosa, and the surf town of Dominical.

Gourmet Restaurants

Enjoy the flavors from world famous restaurants such as Seba's & Sibu as well as other local favorites.

Nightlife

Make friends and have some late night fun at local venues Coco Bongo, La Colonia, and Whale Tail Brewery.
